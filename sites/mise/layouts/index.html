A note on what we keep

We see your data once. Then we forget it.

When you drop a file, we send the rows to a small inference service that decides which widgets to render and where. The response is the layout — a recipe, not a copy. The rows go nowhere else. We don't have a database for them. There's no my dashboards page hiding your data on our servers, because there are no servers holding your data.
